About

This club was formed to help young adults at Walton High School explore the areas of the world that you can't discover sitting at a school desk. A lot of careers in modern day society include jobs that require technological skill. Our vision is to have bright young minds explore the technological world. Out goal being to help Walton students explore the field of technology and choose ideas that they can pursue in the future. This club has a lot of benefits for the students at Walton High School.
